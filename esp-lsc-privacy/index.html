Privacy Policy for ESP Local Server Control

At Pranasucitra, your privacy is a priority. This Privacy Policy explains how your data is collected, used, and protected when you use our application ("ESP Local Server Control") available on the Google Play Store.

1. Data Collection

Our App uses a webview plugin to interact with a local ESP32 web server for controlling devices. To provide this functionality:

  • Wi-Fi Access: The App requires access to your deviceâ€™s Wi-Fi settings to connect to the ESP32 local server.
  • SSID and Password: The App may prompt you to enter your Wi-Fi SSID and password for connection purposes. This information is used solely for establishing a direct connection to the ESP32 web server and is not stored or shared.
  • Access to Local IP: The App connects to the local IP address 192.168.4.1 to communicate with the ESP32 server.

2. Data Usage

  • The SSID, password, and local IP 192.168.4.1 are temporarily used to configure and communicate with the ESP32 server.
  • No data entered in the App is transmitted to external servers or third-party services.

3. Data Storage

The App does not store any sensitive data, including Wi-Fi credentials, beyond the duration of its operation. All interactions occur locally between your device and the ESP32 server.

4. Permissions

The App requires the following permissions:

  • Access to Wi-Fi settings: To connect to the local ESP32 server.
  • Access to Local IP: To communicate with the ESP32 server at 192.168.4.1.

5. Third-Party Access

We do not share, sell, or disclose any user data to third parties.

6. Security

We take all reasonable steps to ensure your data remains secure. However, because data transfer occurs locally and not over the internet, the security of your data largely depends on your local network configuration.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices. Any updates will be made available within the App and on its Google Play Store listing.
